Professional Profile

Dr. Parth Jani

Hybrid Neurosurgeon

Dr. Parth Jani is a renowned Hybrid Neurosurgeon specializing in advanced brain, spine, and neurovascular surgery. A Double Gold Medalist, he completed his M.Ch. in Neurosurgery with distinction and pursued advanced fellowships in Cerebrovascular Neurosurgery at PGIMER Chandigarh and Stroke & Neurointervention.

With 3,500+ successful neurosurgeries and extensive experience in both microsurgical and endovascular procedures, he is recognized for managing some of the most complex neurological conditions.

His expertise includes brain and spine tumors, brain aneurysms, acute stroke interventions, mechanical thrombectomy, AVMs, pituitary surgery, cerebrovascular disorders, and minimally invasive neurosurgery.
