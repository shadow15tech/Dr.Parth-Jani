Dr. Parth Jani

M.Ch. Neurosurgery (Double Gold Medalist)

Recognized as one of India's most promising Hybrid Neurosurgeons, Dr. Jani merges masterful microsurgical techniques with cutting-edge endovascular interventions.

Completing his M.Ch. in Neurosurgery with absolute distinction, he pursued highly coveted advanced fellowships in Cerebrovascular Neurosurgery at PGIMER Chandigarh and Stroke & Neurointervention. This dual-expertise allows him to treat the most intricate neurological conditions from both inside and outside the blood vessels.

Currently serving at HCG Hospitals, Ahmedabad, his clinical focus spans complex brain and spine tumors, brain aneurysms, acute stroke interventions, mechanical thrombectomy, and minimally invasive neurosurgery.
